# Project Context — KIR Handbook Website

## Core Direction

This project is a modern Indonesian vehicle inspection handbook website focused on:

- KIR
- PKB
- Uji Berkala Kendaraan Bermotor

The website is designed as a **practical handbook**, not a generic SEO blog.

### Target Audience

- Vehicle owners
- Truck and bus operators
- Workshop personnel
- Fleet administrators
- Dishub field staff

### Main Objectives

- Explain KIR regulations and field implementation clearly and practically
- Reduce bureaucratic confusion
- Preserve operational and field knowledge
- Build a long-term technical/public-service knowledge archive

---

## Editorial Style

### Tone

- Calm
- Technical
- Practical
- Documentary-like
- Experience-driven

### Avoid

- Clickbait
- Corporate marketing tone
- AI-generic SEO filler
- Excessive bureaucratic wording
- Overly formal legal writing

### Prioritize

- Real operational implications
- Practical explanations
- Field experience
- Clear procedures
- Human-readable regulation interpretation

---

## Writing Structure

Common article sections:

- **Menurut Regulasi**
- **Di Lapangan**
- **Catatan Praktis**
- **Perlu Diperhatikan**

### Writing Characteristics

- Explain *why* something matters
- Mention operational consequences
- Use simple Indonesian for technical topics
- Treat regulations as contextual references, not copied legal text
- Focus on usability in real inspection environments

### Tone Example

Bad:

> “Layanan uji berkala kendaraan bermotor merupakan implementasi…”

Good:

> “Banyak kendaraan gagal uji bukan karena kerusakan besar, tapi karena detail kecil yang sering diabaikan sebelum masuk jalur uji.”
